About Us

We are a group of dog lovers who established this business as a way to supplement our way of life, rather than simply a profit avenue. We want to help you understand your dog's needs and communicate with them in a way both of you can understand. Members of our organization have expertise in trick training, agility, and obedience. We also have a behaviorist on staff who specializes in rehabilitating reactive or aggressive animals.

We prefer positive training methods and encourage our members to do so as well. Any use of aggression toward animals will not be tolerated in the classroom. If you're accustomed to using aversive training techniques, please request a private consultation before registering for group classes.
