Private Lessons

Private lessons are conducted on an as-needed basis. Our trainers can help with complex behavioral issues such as resource guarding, reactivity, inappropriate elimination, and unwanted barking. We can also give you personalized instruction to better reach your obedience goals, compete in AKC-sanctioned competitions, or simply improve your bond with your dog. To better assist you, please fill out the request form below and a member will reply to ask clarifying questions and schedule the services most pertinent to your needs.
